Medical Leave Application Form:

The Medical Leave Application can be collected by a student for a long leave of illness and the percentage of attendance is reduced than needed
This approval can reduce the student mandatory attendance to 70% for the semester.

Procedure
These form’s can be collected from the Office of every department’s.

This form can be used obtain the MEDICAL LEAVE  for a Student.

Specify the reason for your medical leave (illness, injury, etc.).

Mention the date when your medical condition started.

Attach any relevant medical documents (such as doctor’s notes or prescriptions).

Forms may require approvals from specific authorities:

   Your academic advisor or department head
   The college or university health center
   Any other relevant personnel

The current date when you’re submitting the form
