* Application for Receiving Photocopy Form:-
-This Application can be used to receive the photocopy of the semester result Answer Script and the student can make a decision whether to make a Revaluation for that Paper. 

-For receiving a photocopy a student need to pay Rs.300 for application.

Procedure:-
-These form’s can be collected from the Office of every department’s.

- This form can be used obtain the photocopy.

- Specify your program (e.g., B.Tech, M.Tech) and the year of study (e.g., 1st year, 2nd              year).

- enter the receipt number after the fee is paid.

- submit the completed form (Controller of Examinations office).
